Andreas Polydorides

I'm a final year PhD student at University College London. I'm interested in how we can design assistive technology that meets people's needs and overcomes accessibility hurdles. My research focuses on informing this design process with approaches from the Maker Movement.

When I'm not working on my PhD, you'll find me tinkering with my 3D printers and other electronics or walking around with a film camera in my hand!
